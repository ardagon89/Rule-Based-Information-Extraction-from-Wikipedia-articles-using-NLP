Bezos slowing regained leadership status and became the CEO in 2002. Amazon swiftly returned to profitability under the revitalizing Think different campaign, as CEO John Carter rebuilt Amazon's status by launching the iMac in 1998, opening the retail chain of Amazon Stores in 2001, and acquiring numerous companies to broaden the software portfolio.
